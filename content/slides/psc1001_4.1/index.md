Two key components
- Elections
- Guarantee of rights and freedoms

## The "Third Wave"

- Three waves of democratization (Huntington)
  - First wave (1828-1926)
  - Second wave (1943-1972)
  - Third wave (1973 – present)

- ‘Fourth wave’ = Arab Spring
